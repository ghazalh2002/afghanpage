هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

He who has a taller roof, gets more snow

More power brings more responsibility and problems.

آب که یک‌جا بماند، می‌گندد

Stagnant water stinks

Lack of movement or progress causes decay.

باد آورده را باد می‌برد

What comes with the wind, goes with the wind

Easy gains are easily lost.

Reflection of Afghan Wisdom — 2025
